BIRYANI

This flavorful and aromatic biryani is a perfect blend of spices, rice, and tender meat, creating a delicious and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces

  • 1 cup yogurt

  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced

  • 2 tomatoes, chopped

  • 1/4 cup cooking oil

  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)

  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves

  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves

  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)

  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder

  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder

  • 1 teaspoon garam masala

  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Rinse the basmati rice in cold water and soak it for 30 minutes.

  2. In a large pot, heat oil and ghee. Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.

  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.

  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.

  5. Stir in tomatoes, yogurt, mint leaves, coriander leaves, and spices.

  6. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.

  7. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Mix well to coat the rice with the masala.

  8. Add water (ratio: 1 cup rice to 2 cups water), salt, and bring it to a boil.

  9. Reduce the heat, cover the pot, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.

  10. Fluff the biryani with a fork, garnish with fried onions, and serve hot.
