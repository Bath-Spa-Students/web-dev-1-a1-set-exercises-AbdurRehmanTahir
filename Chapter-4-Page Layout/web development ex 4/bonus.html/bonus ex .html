Week 1

  • In the first week of the module, our teacher gave a brief overview of the curriculum, handed out the first assignment, and greeted a new student. The instructor reassured us that the task was doable, establishing a climate of accessibility and preparedness for the voyage ahead.

Week 2

  • We started Assessment 1 in the second week after obtaining comprehensive guidance from our instructor. We carried out the activity as she instructed. Her comments, which validated its perfection, strengthened our comprehension and inspired continued excellence.

Week 3

  • This past week, our module instructor made us aware of an approaching test that is set for the first class of the third week. With an emphasis on HTML and CSS basics, the instructor included students in interactive learning by asking one to show off their coding abilities and offering remedies for mistakes.
