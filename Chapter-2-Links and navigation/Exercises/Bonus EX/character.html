Characters

  • Caitlin Snow / Killer Frost: A bioengineer who can manipulate ice and cold. Portrayed by Danielle Panabaker.
  • Joe West: Barry's adoptive father and a detective in the Central City Police Department. Portrayed by Jesse L. Martin.
  • Wally West / Kid Flash: Iris's brother who gains super-speed abilities. Portrayed by Keiynan Lonsdale.
  • Ralph Dibny / Elongated Man: A private investigator with the ability to stretch his body. Portrayed by Hartley Sawyer.
